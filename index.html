Frequently Asked Questions

What is Cryptocurrency?

Cryptocurrency is a form of digital money that gets its value from community use and adoption. Transactions are peer-to-peer ( no middleman ) and you are the bank. You access your cryptocurrency ( money ) with a private key that is generated when you create your wallet.

Who owns MasterStake?

MasterStake is owned by each and everyone of its holders and adopters - essentially its COMMUNITY. MasterStake is a cryptocurrency, a decentralized form of digital money. 

If something happens to my wallet who do I go to?

That depends on what wallet you use. Anyone can create a MasterStake Wallet or add MasterStake to its existing wallet software. If you are referring to one of the wallets listed under our official wallet page there are a few places you can go.

Desktop Wallets >>

  • Join the Telegram channel and discuss any issues you are having here.

Mobile Wallets >>

What is a Masternode?

Masternode is defined as a governing hub in some cryptocurrency networks. It requires an initial collateral of tokens (or a “stake”) to operate. You can acquire the necessary collateral for a Masternode by accumulating MasterStakes or by purchasing a fully hosted and managed Masternode .

If I lose my private key can I recover access to my MasterStake?

Only with our official mobile wallets. If you have a paper wallet or a desktop wallet and lose access we cannot recover your funds. 

How can I make money with MasterStake

MasterStake is a form of money. You can trade MasterStake on various markets and possibly gain or lose whatever currency is traded against it ( ie. USD ). You can earn MORE MasterStake by staking (saving) and earning interest or earning more coins with a Masternode.

Are my MasterStakes safe?

As long as you do not share your PRIVATE key with others it is very secure. The only thing you can share publicly or with others is your PUBLIC key ( essentially your Wallet Address ). 

What is MasteStake based on?

The same thing many currencies ( especially cryptocurrencies ) are backed by - the community that believes in its value.

What is the benefit of MasterStake?

MasterStake has many benefits, especially for economically disadvantaged communities. Here are a few:

  • It's decentralized, so no matter what happens to a country, nation, state, or locale - commerce between the people can still continue without disruption.
  • It gives us a viable OPTION in dire economic times.
  • It allows us to track and view our collective spending power
  • Its building an affinity network and businesses.
